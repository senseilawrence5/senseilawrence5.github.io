Who we are

Our dojo Cobra Kai is one of the best dojos. We believe that because we take children and adults from any age, how them the way of the fist and make them strong fighters. Important info

Lessons are held in reseda every Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday. For begginers the lessons are from 3:00 to 5:00 and for intermidiate from 5:00 to 7:00. The lessons are 30$ per month. Keep in mind that everyone has a chance to come to our dojo and be a student for a day for free.
